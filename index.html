Common questions

  • How much does it cost?

    + -
    Syncing two devices is free, to sync more we charge $4.99 /m ($29.99/YR -50%) or buy forever at $79.99

  • How does the sync work?

    + -
    Data is syncronized automatically between devices offline via BlueTooth. Also works in stand by.

  • How does backups work?

    + -
    We support 2 types of backups, iCloud or USB Thumb, Securely encrypted with your own password

  • How can I import my passwords?

    + -
    Import from LastPass, Dashlane, 1password, keeper, iCloud passwords or check out KeyChain export

  • How is security and encryption handled?

    + -
    The network uses E2EE and the database is secured with 256-bit encryption. Private keys are stored in secure enclave. We also encrypt user app settings to avoid any chance of someone physically hacking into the app.

  • Why not use 1password or KeyChain?

    + -
    You will always be at risk of compromise if someone gets access to your master password, or someone with access to the the cloud leaks your data.

  • Why the name Sentry?

    + -
    We wanted to create a secure sanctuary for our digital keys. The definition of a sentry is: "The soldier who stands guard".
