1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. In our case, they do not collect personal data.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of our website and its features. They enable you to navigate the website and use essential functions, such as accessing secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect anonymized information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit and any errors you encounter. These cookies help us understand and improve the performance of our website.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make, such as language preferences and customizations. They enhance your user experience by providing personalized features.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use analytics tools to collect anonymized information about how our website is used. This information helps us analyze and improve our website's performance and content.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party cookies provided by trusted partners. These cookies may be used for various purposes, including analytics and improving the functionality of our website. These partners do not collect personal data through our website's cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to accept or reject cookies, delete stored cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. Disabling cookies may affect your experience but will not compromise your personal data as we do not collect such information.
